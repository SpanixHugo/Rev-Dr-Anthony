About

Rev. Dr. Anthony Okechukwu Ezekwu is a Catholic priest, scholar, writer, teacher, mentor, counselor, and an advocate of justice and peace. He has strong professional skills in philosophy, human management, leadership, research, public speaking, mentorship, counseling, writing, and storytelling.

He is a passionate priest and Post-Doctoral researcher deeply committed to youth development and charity work. Through retreats and seminars, he inspires both youths and organizations with eloquent, story-filled talks. Dynamic and multi-talented, he has authored numerous books and articles including:

  • Religion, Politics and Ethnic Games in Nigeria
  • Transformed or Conformed
  • The Hidden World of Autism
  • Crisis of Morality Among Youths
  • Dreaming in the Morning

In addition, he has released two captivating musical albums that reflect his creativity and passion for art.

Beyond his academic and pastoral engagements, he enjoys reading novels, sports, music, and travelling.
